See features
Library

Your NAS, on your phone

On the same network, TuneSync finds your media server automatically — configure once and you're in. Pick which folders to index and that's all it includes. Play as soon as it's done.

  • WebDAV / SMB / NFS / FTP / DAAP protocols
  • Bonjour auto-discovery of LAN servers
  • Folder-level indexing — only what you choose
  • Mix local folders and remote servers in one library
Playback

Lyrics highlight as the song plays

The player scrolls the lyrics with the song, highlighting the current line. Tap a line to jump there. The background color is pulled from the artwork, and you can switch to plain-text reading mode when there's no timing.

  • Full-screen / mini-player, switch anytime
  • Synced lyrics: auto-scroll, tap-to-seek, drag-to-browse
  • Queue, loop, shuffle, speed, AirPlay
  • Sleep timer and background playback
Karaoke

Drop the vocals, sing it yourself

TuneSync splits a song into vocal and accompaniment tracks right on your phone. Lower the original vocal, sing along, and the mic records what you sing. The first split takes a moment; after that it's cached for next time.

  • On-device Core ML separation (MDX / DTTNet)
  • Live vocal-level control with a 3-2-1 countdown
  • Mic recording with input/output latency compensation
  • Saves to “My Recordings” for playback and sharing
AI assistant

Your key, your model

Drop in your own API key and it works — OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Zhipu, DeepSeek all have presets, or fill in a custom endpoint or local Ollama. The AI DJ keeps playing based on what you listen to, and can clean up messy file names.

  • 40+ provider presets + custom OpenAI-compatible endpoints
  • Instant / reasoning model roles, auto-selected per task
  • AI DJ with three autonomy levels: preview / one-tap / auto-continue
  • Tool calling: web search, album art, lyrics, playback and playlist control
Discover

When you don't know what to play

See charts from a few platforms on the home screen. When you're stuck, TuneSync suggests based on what you usually play. Hear a good song out there? Open recognition and it tells you what it is.

  • Apple Music / Bilibili / YouTube / Last.fm multi-source charts
  • Suggestions based on what you usually play
  • ShazamKit recognition with browsable history

What it does

Library, playback, organizing, singing along — all in one app.
